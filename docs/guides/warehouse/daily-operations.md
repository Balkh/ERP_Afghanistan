# Warehouse Staff Guide

## Overview
This guide covers daily warehouse operations for inventory management.

## Daily Workflow

### Morning Tasks
1. Check incoming deliveries (Purchase → Receive)
2. Verify stock levels
3. Check for low-stock alerts

### Receiving Products
1. Go to Purchases → Receive
2. Select purchase order
3. Verify quantities match
4. Confirm receipt
5. Stock automatically added to inventory

### Stock Movement
1. Go to Inventory → Stock Movement
2. Select movement type (IN/OUT/ADJUSTMENT)
3. Choose product and batch
4. Enter quantity
5. Add notes

## Key Operations

### Checking Stock
1. Go to Inventory → Products
2. Search by name, SKU, or barcode
3. View available quantities per warehouse

### Viewing Batches
1. Go to Inventory → Batches
2. See expiry dates
3. Track batch quantities
4. Identify near-expiry items

### Stock Transfer Between Warehouses
1. Go to Inventory → Transfer
2. Select source warehouse
3. Select destination warehouse
4. Choose products and quantities
5. Confirm transfer

## Important Alerts

### Low Stock Warning
- Check Dashboard for low-stock items
- Report to management for reordering

### Expiry Warning
- Check for items expiring within 30 days
- Report to sales team for priority selling

## Common Tasks

### Find Product by Barcode
1. In any product screen
2. Use barcode scanner or manual entry
3. Product details displayed

### Check Product Quantity
1. Go to Inventory → Products
2. Click on product name
3. View batch details and quantities

## Warning Section
⚠️ **Important:**
- Always verify quantities before confirming
- Report any discrepancies immediately
- Keep warehouse organized

## Common Mistakes
1. Entering wrong quantities
2. Selecting wrong product
3. Forgetting to confirm transactions
