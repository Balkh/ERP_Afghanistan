# Accountant Guide

## Overview
This guide covers financial operations and accounting workflows.

## Daily Tasks

### Morning Review
1. Check trial balance
2. Review cash flow
3. Check pending payments

### End of Day
1. Verify all sales posted
2. Check bank reconciliation
3. Review journal entries

## Key Financial Workflows

### Sales Accounting
1. Sales invoice automatically creates journal entries
2. Debit: Accounts Receivable (or Cash)
3. Credit: Sales Revenue
4. View in Accounting → Journal

### Purchase Accounting
1. Purchase receipt creates journal entries
2. Debit: Inventory (or Expense)
3. Credit: Accounts Payable
4. View in Accounting → Journal

### Payment Recording
1. Go to Payments → Receive (for customer payments)
2. Go to Payments → Pay (for supplier payments)
3. Select payment method (Cash, Bank, Hawala, Mobile)
4. System automatically records transactions

## Financial Reports

### Trial Balance
- Location: Accounting → Reports → Trial Balance
- Shows all account balances
- Debits must equal credits

### Profit & Loss
- Location: Accounting → Reports → P&L
- Revenue minus expenses
- Filter by date range

### Balance Sheet
- Location: Accounting → Reports → Balance Sheet
- Assets = Liabilities + Equity
- Shows financial position

### Cash Flow
- Location: Accounting → Reports → Cash Flow
- Operating, investing, financing activities

## Account Management

### Chart of Accounts
- View all accounts: Accounting → Chart of Accounts
- Account types: Asset, Liability, Revenue, Expense, Equity
- Do not modify account codes without reason

### Journal Entry Manual Entry
1. Go to Accounting → Journal Entry
2. Select entry type
3. Add debit and credit lines
4. Must balance (debits = credits)
5. Save as draft or post immediately

## Currency Operations

### Exchange Rates
1. Go to Accounting → Currencies
2. Set exchange rates between AFN and USD
3. Used for multi-currency invoices

### Hawala Tracking
- Hawala is common in Afghanistan
- Track hawala settlements in payments
- Record reference numbers

## Important Alerts

### Trial Balance Not Balancing
- Check for unbalanced journal entries
- Verify all entries posted correctly

### Outstanding Receivables
- Monitor aging report
- Follow up on overdue amounts

## Warning Section
⚠️ **Important:**
- Never delete posted journal entries
- Always use reversal entries to correct
- Keep backup of financial reports

## Common Mistakes
1. Forgetting to post journal entries
2. Entering unbalanced entries
3. Not reconciling bank accounts
